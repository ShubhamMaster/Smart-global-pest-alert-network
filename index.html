Empowering Farmers to Combat Pests

The Smart Global Pest Alert Network is a global platform that leverages AI, satellite data, and crowd-sourced reports to predict pest migration patterns and issue early warnings to farmers. It helps farmers prevent crop damage, protect yields, and adopt proactive pest management strategies.
