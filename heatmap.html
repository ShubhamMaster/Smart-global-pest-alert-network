How to Use the Heatmap

The heatmap visualizes pest activity based on real-time data collected from farmers and monitoring systems. Areas colored in red indicate a high risk of pest outbreaks, while yellow areas signify medium risk. Green areas indicate low risk, and blue areas show no current threat.

Click on any region to view detailed information, including:

  • Recent pest sightings
  • Species identified
  • Recommended actions

Alert: Recent locust sightings reported in the Central Valley region. Farmers are advised to monitor crops closely and prepare necessary pest control measures.
